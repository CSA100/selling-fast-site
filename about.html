Selling Fast App aims to provide an easy yet effective way for Shopify Merchants to social proof their online stores.

The app allows you to display the quantity sold for each product on your online store. This provides a subtle yet effective social proof. Your customers can gain confidence that your product is tried, tested and loved by many. It can also showcase how trendy your products are!

Selling Fast provides loads of customisation features to suit the look and feel of your brand.

Install our app on the Shopify app store today!
